# Project Brief — PhoneValue

## What we are building
PhoneValue is a web-based valuation platform that bridges the gap between individual smartphone owners and local phone shops. Users complete a standardized "health check" questionnaire for their device to receive estimated trade-in or sale values from nearby retailers in the local market.

## Who it is for
- **End Customers**: Individuals looking to sell or trade-in their used phones without visiting multiple physical stores for quotes.
- **Phone Shop Owners**: Businesses looking for a steady stream of verified used inventory and pre-qualified leads.

## What users can do
- **Device Questionnaire**: Input phone model, storage capacity, battery health, and physical condition.
- **Valuation**: Receive instant price estimates in **ALL** or **EUR**.
- **Lead Generation**: View participating shops and their specific offers based on the device report.
- **Shop Browsing**: Identify which local shops are offering the best rates for specific models.

## Main screens
- **Landing/Search**: Select phone brand and model to start the process.
- **Condition Questionnaire**: Multi-step form assessing battery health, storage, and level of physical damage.
- **Value Results**: Comparison of offers from different shops based on the calculated condition.
- **Shop Detail/Contact**: Location and contact information for the chosen retailer.

## Future additions (not in MVP)
- User accounts and history of previous valuations.
- Real-time chat or bidding system between shops and users.
- Automated hardware diagnostics via a mobile app.
- In-app payment or deposit system for securing a price.
- Integration with map services for real-time shop directions.
